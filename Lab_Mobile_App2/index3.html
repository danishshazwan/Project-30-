Your privacy matters. This policy explains clearly what data Lumut Dash collects, why we collect it, and how it is protected. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

1 Information we collect

When you register and use the Lumut Dash school portal, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your full name and email address provided at registration.
  • Authentication data — your password, stored securely and never in plain readable form on a production server.
  • Usage data — pages visited, filter preferences, and session activity within the portal.
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, and IP address for security logging.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as identification numbers, financial information, or health records through this portal.

2 How we use your information

The information we collect is used solely to operate and improve the school portal. Specifically:

  • To authenticate your identity and grant access to the portal.
  • To display personalised content such as your name in the navigation bar.
  • To remember your preferences (e.g. last selected news filter).
  • To send password reset codes to your registered email address when requested.
  • To detect and prevent unauthorised access or abuse of the portal.

3 How we store your data

User account data and session preferences are currently stored in your browser's localStorage for demonstration purposes. In a production deployment, data would be stored on a secured school server with the following protections:

  • Passwords hashed using a strong algorithm (e.g. bcrypt) — never stored in plain text.
  • Data encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Access restricted to authorised school administrators only.
  • Regular security audits and backups.

Data is retained for the duration of your enrolment and deleted within 90 days of account closure.

4 Sharing of information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with outside parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • School administration — authorised staff may access account records for school management purposes.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or a lawful order from a Malaysian authority.
  • Security incidents — if necessary to investigate a breach or protect the safety of students.

If you choose to sign in with Google, your authentication is handled directly by Google under their own privacy policy. We only receive your name and email address from Google.

5 Cookies and local storage

Lumut Dash uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to save your session and preferences. This data stays on your own device and is never transmitted to external servers in the demo version.

You can clear this data at any time by logging out or clearing your browser's site data in your browser settings.

6 Student and minor privacy

Many of our users are students under the age of 18. We are committed to protecting the privacy of minors in accordance with Malaysian law:

  • We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the portal.
  • Student data is not used for advertising or commercial profiling.
  • Parents or guardians may request access to or deletion of their child's account data by contacting the school.
If you are a parent or guardian with concerns about your child's data, please contact the school office directly.

7 Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion — request that your account and data be deleted.
  • Portability — receive your data in a commonly used format.
  • Objection — object to certain uses of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact the school office using the details in Section 9.

8 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of the portal after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
